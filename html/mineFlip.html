How to play

The goal of this game is to flip every tile on the board without hitting a mine. There are 2 rows which give you information. The right-most column tells you the sum of the tiles of the row directly to their left and the amount of mines in it as well. The bottom-most row tells you the same information but about the columns directly above instead. Use these pieces of information and solve the puzzle.

Fairness

This game is part luck, part skill. Not every game has 0% luck involved. Your odds of solving vary from game to game and sometimes there is no skill involved whatsoever. Good luck player

Payout system

The profit margin in this game vary from 2.5x to 3x the invested amount of money. The profit is calculated by summing all the points on the board and dividing the result by 10.
